about

Raised on the westside of Detroit, G. King captivates audiences with his thought provoking lyricism that he suggests is better consumed in the evening time.

King is committed to bringing his listeners substance masked over various instrumentation taking inspiration all over the spectrum from artists such as Kendrick Lamar, Sean Leon, Eli Sostre, and King Krule.

G. King has opened for acts such as Audio Push, Snow the Product, Sage Francis, and aims to elevate his musical reach since moving to New York. 100Fold return is the mantra.
